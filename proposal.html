Background

The Sydney Children's Hospitals Foundation is an independant health promotion charity established in 1986 aiming to raise money to support sick children and their families

Key Statistics

The Sydney Childrens Hospitals Foundation has contributed around $200 million to support families with sick children.
This foundation supports the following Sydney Children's Hospital Networks:

  • Kid's Research
  • Bear Cottage
  • The Newborn and Paediatric Emergency Transport Service
  • Sydney Children's Hospital, Randwick
  • The Children's Hospital at Westmead

Ideation

To start developing my website design I first wanted to throw together some ideas with fonts, shapes, and colors just to think of some themes in the form of moodboards that I could use for my website development. As well as this, I started some initial ideation of the layout and design of my website through some rough sketches which put my thoughts onto paper from which I could expand and move onto developing wireframes and mockups of the design I chose.


Mood Boards


Moodboard #1

My first moodboard encorporates a brighter color theme with a funky style. After consideration of this style I do not wish to use this in my website as it doesn't really catch my eye as an aesthetic design, as it lacks unity among the different fonts colors and shapes.


Moodboard #2

With this moodboard I went for a much quieter design with greens and purples trying to convey a sense of empathy. Although I do like this theme over the previous I still don't feel like this is the right design for the charity I have chosen.



Moodboard #3

I really like the color theme of this moodboard as it seems sophisticated whilst still bright conveying a sense of hope. I like the symetry and geometry in this moodboard and I think that is something that I will encorporate in my next steps.


Sketches

To further develop my design and explore my concept I have created some initial sketches to ideate some website layouts and designs.

Homepage

In these sketches I tried to encorporate the themes in moodboard 3 using lots of geometery in the layout. These sketches are very simple but convey the most important points that I wish to convey to my target audience with a great salience brought to the key words such as 'donate' and 'sign up'. I didn't want to make the websites too confussing or compact otherwise it would draw away from the main message I am trying to convey.
